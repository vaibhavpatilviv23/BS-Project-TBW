Bootstrap is a CSS framework that helps devs to make a website in short period of time.

The queries are already written for most of the devices like mobiles, tablets, desktops and laptops. It just requires a compatible browser, although most of the browsers are compatible.

Bootstrap framework is available for everyone for free. Anyone can make use of it by adding it to your projects, that is also with multiple ways.
